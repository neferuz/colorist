01 / Info

Info

What I do

I work as a colorist, bringing stories to life through color. I enhance mood, emotion, and atmosphere in commercials, documentaries, music videos, YouTube shows, and short films. My work focuses on creating visually compelling, cinematic, and professional imagery.

My background

I have been a colorist for over 2 years, during which I have worked on 30+ commercials, 10+ documentary films, as well as various music videos, YouTube shows, and short films.

My approach

I see color as a world. Through it, I convey joy and sadness, richness and poverty, hope and hopelessness. I pay attention to tonal balance, contrast, and emotion, aiming to support the story without overpowering it.

Career

Over the last two years, I have built a portfolio of commercial, documentary, and music projects. Each project reflects my dedication to clean, cinematic, and story-driven color grading, while adapting to the needs of different genres and platforms.
